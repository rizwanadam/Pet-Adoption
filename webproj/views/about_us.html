ABOUT US
Pet adoption is the process of taking responsibility for a pet that a previous owner has abandoned or released to a shelter or rescue organization. Common sources for adoptable pets are animal shelters and rescue groups.

The Pet.find portal supports the shelters and rescue groups that post their adoptable pets on Pet.find. Our aim is to prevent the euthanasia of adoptable pets and to provide a safe, loving and caring home for them.

Pet.find can be accessed by people from anywhere across the country. The pet lovers can register themselves on the platform to search and adopt a pet. The user also has an option to view different pictures of the pet, know the temperament of the pet and even ask queries. It is a very user interactive portal.

Well, what are you waiting for? Give a pet a loving home using Pet.find!
